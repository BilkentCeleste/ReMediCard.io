About Us

Our application integrates the essential features of widely-used flashcard applications available in the market, designed to alleviate the challenges of memorization encountered by medical students through the application of computer vision and machine learning technologies.

  • 🔍 User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use.
  • 💡 Advanced Technologies: Utilizing computer vision and machine learning.
  • 🎓 Comprehensive Solution: Targeting specific burdens in medical studies.

By leveraging these advanced technologies, our additional features aim to provide a comprehensive solution to the specific burdens associated with medical studies.
